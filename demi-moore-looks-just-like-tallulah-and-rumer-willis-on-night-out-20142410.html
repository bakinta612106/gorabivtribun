Demi Moore Looks Just Like Tallulah and Rumer Willis On Night Out

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Ladies’ night!  

From the looks of Demi Moore‘s latest evening o’ fun with daughters Tallulah and Rumer Willis, the ladies could pass for sisters, despite decades of difference in age. 

The cheerful trio attended the Tracy Paul & Company Celebrates Fashion’s Best Kept Secret Torrid event on Thursday, Oct. 23, in West Hollywood. Moore, 51, fit right in with her young crew in a printed short suit, and a messy side braid. Tallulah, 20, looked cool and edgy in a black tuxedo outfit with bright red pumps. While Rumer, 26, stood out in an all-black look with pointy-toe pumps. (Middle daughter Scout Willis, 23, was not at the family outing.)

Tallulah and Moore also went to the Brian Bowen Smith’s Wildlife show afterparty at the De Re Gallery to celebrate the Brian Bowen Smith Wildlife Show in West Hollywood.
