Scott Disick Drinks Detox Tea After Kourtney Kardashian Split: Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Care for some tea? Newly single Scott Disick revealed on Tuesday, July 14, that he's been drinking a detox, dietary tea since his high-profile split from Kourtney Kardashian.

"Staying fresh with @FitTea," Disick, 32, wrote on Instagram in a promotional post, alongside a photo of himself in a black hoodie holding the "28 Day Detox" product. Like his ex, Disick has weight loss on his mind. The product is known to suppress appetite and help with fat burning.

In the accompanying photo, the father of three sports an unkempt beard and has his hair slicked back in a high coiffe as he stares at the camera lens. "Gotta be fresh to stay on the grind," he continued.

Several hours after his detox tea post, Disick shared yet another update on Instagram, this time of his dinner in another promotional post. The reality star, who has millions of followers on his various social media accounts, gets paid an average of $25,000 per promotional social media post.

"This little dish makes me happy," the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star wrote, alongside a shot of his healthy dinner: rice noodles sprinkled with scallions, sprouts, and more. The post, however, appeared to be promotional for the Wine and Dine app, which he tagged in the social media update.

Us Weekly confirmed on July 6, that he and Kardashian, 36, were splitting after nine years together, after Disick was photographed in the South of France partying with his ex-girlfriend Chloe Bartoli. "1 of the only things I'm proud off about myself," the father of Mason, 5, Penny, 3, and Reign, 7 months, wrote alongside a collage of photos of his one and only daughter. "Happybdayp."
