Paula Deen Biography

Paula Deen was born on January 19, 1947. Chef, Emmy-Award-winning TV host, actress, and author best known for her Food Network cooking shows. In Elizabethtown, she starred opposite Orlando Bloom. She quit her job as a bank teller and moved to Georgia with just $200 in her pocket to start catering.
