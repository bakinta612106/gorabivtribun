Coline Creuzot Wears Her Heart On Her Sleeve With New EP

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Coline Creuzot strikes a chord in her listeners with her unapologetic lyricism and an utterly out of this world presence as a vocalist. She’s a three-time Billboard charting musician who spins narratives around her takeaways from heartbreak. Her new EP, Hearts Don’t Break Even, is a lush ride through Creuzot’s transformative past relationships; guiding us through her divine-dipped atmosphere.

Hearts Don’t Break Even is swimming with subtle beats, crescendos of fluttering rhythms, and an all-encompassing vocal at the core of the collection. Creuzot ruminates on intimacy with emotional vulnerability and unabashed delivery. On second track, “That Feeling”, Creuzot croons, “It breaks my heart to break your heart baby – knowing that nobody is really to blame.” Hearts Don’t Break Even is a defining moment in R&B as Creuzot bears her soul in a whirlwind of luxurious sonics.

Coline Creuzot got her start in Houston, Texas. It was here she met the established producer Happy Perez who worked with her to release her single “Give and Take”. The single charted at #1 on Houston Radio Charts and she’s since then moved on to build an exciting career.
