David Harbour Cuddles a Teddy Bear at Thom Browne Fashion Show With Maisie Williams &amp; Danai Gurira

Publish date: 2024-08-17

David Harbour holds on tight to a suited up teddy bear while arriving at the Thom Browne Fall 2022 Fashion Show on Friday (April 29) in New York City.

The Stranger Things actor, who was joined by stars like Maisie Williams and Danai Gurira, passed off the bear to Licorice Pizza‘s Cooper Hoffman for another photo opp.

Other celebrities at the event included Leslie Odom Jr, Jon Batiste, Charles Melton and his new girlfriend Chase Sui Wonders, Amandla Stenberg, Imogen Poots, and Guardians of the Galaxy 3 star Will Poulter.

Ella Emhoff, King Princess, Gossip Girl‘s Evan Mock, Antoni Porowski, couple Iman Shumpert and Teyana Taylor, Simon Rex, Tommy Dorfman, and Tom Sturridge were also seen sitting front row for the presentation.

The new collection is inspired by the Island of Misfit Toys from the classic animated film Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer.

“In a way, I always feel New York is an island of misfit toys,” designer Thom Browne shared with WWD. “It’s a place where you can really be true to yourself and be comfortable. People are so much more accepting and it’s such an inspiring time.”
