Tyler Posey, Fiancee Seana Gorlick Split: Couple Ends Engagement

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Stars, they go through painful breakups too. More than a year after he proposed to childhood girlfriend Seana Gorlick, Teen Wolf star Tyler Posey exclusively revealed to Us Weekly that their engagement and relationship are over. 

“You’re the first interview I’ve said this to. We have broken up,” he told Us at the ASPCA Cocktail Event in honor of Kaley Cuoco-Sweeting and Nikki Reed in the Bel Air neighborhood of L.A., on Wednesday, Oct. 22. “I’m just gonna get it out there because I get sad talking about it. I’m just gonna get it out there now so that hopefully people start hearing about it.”

The actor, 23, who hosted the 2014 Teen Choice Awards with Modern Family’s Sarah Hyland in August, confirmed that the couple had officially split and were not simply postponing their wedding, adding that he’s “okay.” But when asked if he was still friends with his middle-school sweetheart, he replied, “I don’t know.”

Posey initially popped the question in the summer of 2013 on a 12-day vacation to Hawaii. He pulled out the diamond sparkler during dinner on the first night of the trip.
