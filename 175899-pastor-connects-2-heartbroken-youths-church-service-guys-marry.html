Pastor Connects 2 Heartbroken Youths During Church Service: &quot;You Guys Will Marry&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-15
  • A pastor tried to match two heartbroken young people in his church during a service in a viral video
  • As seen in the hilarious video, the man of God asked the duo to hold hands and get each other's phone number
  • The pastor then announced that the two would marry each other, leaving the whole congregation in stitches

An undated video of a pastor trying to help two young people find love in his church has gone viral. There is no better way than to find love through God,a nd these two were blessed with the opportunity.

The video, which was posted on Facebook by user Abdikarim Hussein, showed the hilarious moment when the preacher spotted two youths kneeling in the front row of the church.

Why did the pastor play cupid for two youths?

The pastor, who delivered a healing sermon, asked the two if they had broken hearts.

They both nodded yes, and the pastor proceeded to ask them if they knew each other. A moment these two will never forget.

They shook their heads no, and the pastor decided to introduce them to each other.

“You guys should start talking,” the pastor said, as the congregation erupted in laughter. “Hold her hand,” he added, as he moved the young man closer to the young woman.

The two looked shy and awkward but followed the pastor’s instructions. A love found under the roof of God, one many are praying, lasts a lifetime.

The pastor then asked them to exchange contacts, which they did and announced that they would marry.
