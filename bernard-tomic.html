Bernard Tomic Biography

Bernard Tomic is best known as Professional Tennis Player who has an estimated Net Worth of $4 Million. Australian tennis player who reached the quarterfinals of the 2011 Wimbledon tournament. He was born on October 21,1992, Australia. He competed in the 2012 London Olympics but lost in the first round to Japan's Kei Nishikori. He attended Southport State School where he excelled in tennis, then turned professional in 2008. He was defeated by Novak Djokovic in four sets in the quarterfinals of the 2011 Wimbledon tournament. His father is Croatian and his mother is Bosnian; both left Yugoslavia years before he was born. His younger sister is tennis player Sara Tomic. His zodiac sign is Libra
