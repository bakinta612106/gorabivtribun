Leah Jenner Gives Birth to a Baby Girl! Find Out Her Name

Publish date: 2024-08-21

She's here! Leah Jenner and her husband, Brandon Jenner, are the proud parents of a baby girl, the couple announced via Instagram. Baby Eva James Jenner was born on Wednesday, July 22.

"On Wednesday, July 22nd I became a Mom," Leah posted. "It has without a doubt been the most profoundly beautiful time in my life but also emotionally, spiritually and physically challenging too….and I am so grateful for every second of it. Brandon and I are taking care of our healthy, little baby girl, Eva James Jenner."

Leah, who announced she was expecting in March, kept fans updated via Instagram throughout her pregnancy, making note of how Brandon doted on his wife of three years.

"I feel like Jabba the Hutt, but I have this hot man nurse, soooo . . . " the expectant mom, 32, blogged on July 14 along with a shirtless photo of her smiling hubby. "I feel honored to have this little life growing inside me, and I'm amazed by the power of the female body," she added. "I love being pregnant."

Leah and Brandon, who perform together in their own band, confirmed their baby's gender in June.
