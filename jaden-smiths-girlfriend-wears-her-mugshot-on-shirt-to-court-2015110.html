Jaden Smith's Girlfriend Wears Her Mugshot on Shirt to Court

Publish date: 2024-08-10

56 nights

A photo posted by @sarahfuckingsnyder on Sep 30, 2015 at 5:11pm PDT

Jaden Smith’s girlfriend, Sarah Synder, is probably not winning points with Will and Jada Pinkett Smith. The 19-year-old blonde wore a T-shirt with her mugshot on it to her court hearing on Wednesday, Sept. 30.

“56 nights,” Snyder captioned an Instagram photo of herself posing in the Tee, referring to Future’s track by the same name inspired by DJ Esco’s jail sentence. 

Snyder appeared in court in Bedford, N.Y., for her June 15 arrest. According to Entertainment Tonight, the model was arrested on one count of third-degree larceny for allegedly stealing a $15,995 Hermes bag from a high-end boutique. 

“As of last night, we made it clear to the district attorney’s office that we would not be accepting any offers or any pleas and that no pleas would be entered. The only thing we are interested in is an exoneration and an apology,” Snyder’s attorney, Robert Schuster told ET, in her defense. “At this point we are preparing for a potential trial, whether it is to be tried as a felony or a misdemeanor.”

“My clients are free to express themselves in any way that they choose, and I do not advise them one way or the other on availing themselves of their First Amendment privileges,” Schuster added of the teen’s attire. 

According to the lawyer, Jaden’s girlfriend is innocent and is “erroneously charged in this matter.”

Prior to seemingly mocking her arrest in court, Snyder packed on the PDA with Jaden, 17, at New York Fashion Week, kissing the actor while sitting front row at the Gypsy Sport show on Sept. 15.

The duo started dating earlier this year. “Lit,” she recently captioned a photo of Jaden grabbing her butt on Instagram.
