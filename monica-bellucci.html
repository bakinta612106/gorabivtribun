Monica Bellucci

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Bellucci in 2008, photo by cinemafestival / Shutterstock.com

Birth Name: Monica Anna Maria Bellucci

Place of Birth: Città di Castello, Province of Perugia, Umbria, Italy

Date of Birth: 30 September, 1964

Ethnicity: Italian

Monica Bellucci is an Italian model and actress. Her roles include the films La Riffa, Bram Stoker’s Dracula, The Apartment (1996), Malèna, Brotherhood of the Wolf, Irréversible, Tears of the Sun, The Matrix Reloaded, The Matrix Revolutions, The Brothers Grimm, Shoot ‘Em Up, The Whistleblower, The Wonders, On the Milky Road, The Best Years of a Life, The Man Who Sold His Skin, and Memory.

She is the daughter of Brunella Briganti, a painter, and Pasquale Bellucci, who owned a trucking company. She was raised in Lama, now Selci-Lama, in the comune of San Giustino, by Città di Castello. Monica has two children, including model, media personality, and actress Deva Cassel, with her former husband, French actor Vincent Cassel.
