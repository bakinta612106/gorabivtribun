Southern Charm Star Taylor Ann Greens Brother Worth Dies at 36: Remembering a Life Cut Short

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Taylor Ann Green, a star of Southern Charm, has experienced a heartbreaking loss. Richard Worthington Green, the reality star’s brother, passed away on June 8th at the age of 36, and she is currently in grief. On social media, Taylor’s sister, Catie King, verified the information.

Worthington, often known as Worth, was a project manager for a company that specialised in roofing and repair. His cause of death has not yet been made public. To learn more about the tragedy’s full circumstances, keep reading.

Taylor Ann Green’s Brother Worth Dies at 36

On Monday, Catie King shared the news with the world on Instagram. She wrote, “You are the best big brother I could have ever asked for,” along with a series of pictures of her brother. There will never be a like family again. To the moon and back, I adore you.

One of the images had the statement, “You’re always loved, never forgotten, forever missed.” Taylor has not yet issued a remark regarding the passing of her brother. Shep Rose, who was her ex-boyfriend, remarked, “Absolutely one of a kind,” in a comment on King’s post. a delight to laugh with and know.”

Other than Catie and Taylor, Worth is survived by his parents Rick & Leslie, sister Caroline Evans, paternal grandmother Catherine Sellars Green, maternal grandparents Ralph Marshall Dark, Jr., and Barbara Worthington Dark, ‘adopted’ grandma Melita Burnette, and a number of uncles and aunts.

A Memorial Service will Take Place on Saturday

On June 17th in North Carolina, a memorial service will be held in Worth’s honour. Instead of sending flowers, the family has requested that everyone donate to their preferred organisations. Worth formerly held the position of Senior Project Manager for Charlotte, North Carolina-based Weatherguard Roofing & Restoration.

“I am an expert in hunting and forging new relationships in a competitive market,” his LinkedIn bio stated. I am a seasoned salesperson that is imaginative, creative, results-driven, and a strategic thinker who is committed to finding clients, developing a rapport with that new account, and generating additional income for my firm.

Worth ‘Lived His Life as Fully as Possible,’ Says Obituary

The deceased person’s obituary stated that he was “a beautiful example of how to live life as fully as possible.” According to the article, Worth earned diplomas from East Carolina University and Sunhawk Academy, respectively, in 2005 and 2009.

His character was vivacious, gregarious, and affectionate. A stranger was never known to Worth. After graduating from college, he moved to South Korea and began teaching English as a second language. This is when his love of travel really took off. Playing golf in Ireland with pals was one of his favourite travel experiences. Worth was without a doubt an explorer.

Worth has a deep love for his family. He sincerely loved with all his heart and would find any reason to be with them. The best big brother to his sisters, he was a vibrant and loving son, grandson, and great-grandson who was always there to support and encourage them.
