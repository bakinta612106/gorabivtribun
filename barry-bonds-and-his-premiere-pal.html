BARRY BONDS AND HIS PREMIERE PAL

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Former MLB player Barry Bonds and his daughter Aisha,13, arrived at the premiere of Lionsgate’s and Tyler Perry’s ‘Good Deeds’ held on February 14, 2012 in Los Angeles, California.

Barry has older children Nikolai,22, and Shikari,20, from his first marriage to Susann Branco. Aisha’s mother is Bond’s second wife Liz Watson.
