Miley Cyrus Performs With Sister Noah Cyrus &amp; Brandi Carlile During Miami NYE Special Watch!

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Miley Cyrus put on some amazing performances during NBC’s New Year’s Eve special which she hosted from Miami with Pete Davidson on Friday (December 31).

In between some of her solo performances, the 29-year-old singer hit the stage with her sister Noah Cyrus and Brandi Carlile!

Click inside to watch both of Miley’s duets…

One of the big highlights of the NYE special was Miley teaming up with Brandi for a medley of Brandi‘s “The Story”, and Miley‘s “The Climb.”

Following that, Brandi also performed a retro rendition of Blondie’s 1978 hit “Heart of Glass” and “Total Eclipse of the Heart”.

Later on, Miley and Noah teamed up together to perform “Jolene”, which is a cover of Dolly Parton‘s famous song. Dolly just happens to be Miley‘s godmother.

If you missed it, fans of Miley think she debuted a brand new song during the event!
