Pregnant Kim Kardashian Dresses Baby Bump in Pink Maxi Dress: Photo

Publish date: 2024-09-06

Seeing blue, but wearing pink! Kim Kardashian, who is expecting her second child, a baby boy, with Kanye West, turned heads in a pink maxi dress in L.A. on Friday, Sept. 4.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 34, teamed the cleavage-baring pastel outfit with a cream jacket and heels. She also rocked her 15-carat diamond Lorraine Schwartz engagement ring and kept her luscious dark locks swept back.

Us Weekly exclusively broke the news in May that North West, 2, would be getting a little brother. "Kanye loves Nori more than anything, but to make his world complete he wanted a little boy, an heir. He's overjoyed!" an insider told Us at the time.

A Kardashian pal added: “Kim always wanted two kids, a girl and a boy.”
