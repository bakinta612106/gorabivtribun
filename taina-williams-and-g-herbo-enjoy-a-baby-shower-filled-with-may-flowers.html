TAINA WILLIAMS AND G HERBO ENJOY A BABY SHOWER FILLED WITH 'MAY FLOWERS'

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Taina Williams and G Herbo enjoyed a beautiful baby shower with family and friends in honor of the impending arrival of their baby girl. Keep going to see pictures from the event!

April Belle Photos took amazing candids of the event, which included lots of roses and pink tiered cake designed by Shirin Bakery in Tarzana, California. Butterfly Floral Even Design was responsible for the floral decorations throughout the venue while Sky Balloon Bar, LLC, provided balloons that were nothing short of unique.

Both Taina Williams and G Herbo were happy with the outcome of their baby shower. Taina’s mom, Emily B, coordinated the festivities.

“If there’s one thing we’re gonna do … it’s execute a theme,” Emily said of the outcome of Taina and G Herbo’s baby shower. “When we started planning, Taina wanted ‘April Showers Brings May Flowers,’” the celebrity grandma shared. “Our baby girl is due in May so how perfect,” Emily B exclaimed.

“I knew I had to bring on @pep_ent to bring the vision to life,” Emily continued. “We spoke about ideas and inspiration and boom she killed it! This was beyond my expectations! I was blown away by all the details. So beautiful.”

Taina Williams and G Herbo shared the news of their expectancy last December. The couple took to Instagram on Christmas Eve with the announcement of their family’s expansion. Taina filmed herself sharing the news with G Herbo, which she also shared with her Instagram followers.

“I’m feeling like a should vlog this because I think this may be a thing,” Taina told her social media fans. She then went on to tell her fiance, “I have something to show you.” Williams showed her husband-to-be a positive pregnancy test as a sign that she was indeed pregnant. Taina Williams and G Herbo have been celebrating their future lives as parents of two kids ever since.

Taina and G Herbo’s second child together, a girl, will join the couple’s son, Essex, at home. The little one will make G Herbo a father of three kids.
