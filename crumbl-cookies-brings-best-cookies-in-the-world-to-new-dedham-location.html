Crumbl Brings 'Best Cookies In The World' To New Dedham Location

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Crumbl, a cookie shop known for its trademark pink boxes and a rotating menu of flavors, is opening a new location in Dedham on Friday, July 28. 

Just an opening day was not enough for Crumbl. The new location, located at 747 Providence Highway, will celebrate its opening for a whole week.

Customers can choose from six of the dessert shop's 200+ weekly rotating flavors. The extensive menu of flavors includes award-winning milk chocolate chip, cornbread, s'mores, key lime pie, caramel popcorn, and many more. 

The new location will be the third store for owners Pam and Matthew Arias, who live on the South Shore with their three kids. 

"Our family is excited to open our third store and continue sharing the positivity and joy that Crumbl Cookies can bring to a community," said the couple. 

Crumbl has its sights on yet another Massachusetts spot and is hoping to open a Dartmouth location in fall 2023. 

Customers can order in person for the first five business days of the Dedham store's grand opening and can order for delivery, curbside pickup, catering, and nationwide shipping starting Wednesday, August 2.  

The Dedham store will be open from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. on weekdays and 8 a.m. to 12 a.m. on Fridays and Saturdays.
