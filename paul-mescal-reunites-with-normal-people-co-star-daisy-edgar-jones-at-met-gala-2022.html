Paul Mescal Reunites with Normal People Co-Star Daisy Edgar-Jones at Met Gala 2022

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Paul Mescal and Daisy Edgar-Jones are together again!

The Normal People co-stars posed together on the red carpet at the 2022 Met Gala on Monday evening (May 2) at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City.

For the event, Paul, 26, rocked a mustache with an all-black suit while Daisy, 23, wore a sparkling, silver flipper-inspired dress.

Also in attendance was Paul‘s girlfriend, singer Phoebe Bridgers, who dazzled in a diamond-covered dress. She attended the event with designer Jonathan Simkhai.

Make sure you check out JustJared.com‘s List of Favorite Outfits from tonight’s Met Gala here!

FYI: Paul is wearing a Cartier watch and jewelry. Daisy is wearing an Oscar de la Renta dress, Christian Louboutin shoes, and Tiffany & Co. jewelry. Phoebe is wearing a Jonathan Simkhai dress with Cartier jewelry.
