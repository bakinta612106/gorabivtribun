Adele Visits Site of Grenfell Tower Fire in London to Offer Support

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Adele is another star who has offered support to those who have been affected by the fire in Grenfell Tower.

The fire broke out in London on Wednesday and so far, it has been confirmed that 17 have died with more missing or yet to be identified.

The 24-storey residential tower caught fire in the early hours of the morning and burned for over 10 hours with hundreds of firefighters battling the blaze.

Lots of performers and celebrities voiced their support by highlighting the ways people could help out the victims.

Adele quietly offered her own support by going down to the site with her husband and comforting the victims.

The star was clearly distraught by the tragic event as she consoled the survivors at the vigil.

Eyewitnesses at the scene say that the singer was asking if anyone needed anything and tried to calm those affected.
