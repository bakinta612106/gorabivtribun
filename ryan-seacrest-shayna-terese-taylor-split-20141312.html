Ryan Seacrest, Shayna Terese Taylor Split

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Seacrest out! Ryan Seacrest and his girlfriend of over a year Shayna Terese Taylor have called it quits, the radio host confirms to Us Weekly.

While chatting with Us at Z100’s Jingle Ball on Friday, Dec. 12, the host, who turns 40 on Dec. 24, admitted, “I am single.”

Seacrest, who previously dated Julianne Hough for two years, first stepped out with the model and personal trainer in November 2013 on vacation in Uruguay. According to a source at the time, "Ryan was looking to date again [following his March 2013 split with Hough], and [friends] thought he and Shayna would get along great!"

Despite mainly keeping quiet about their relationship, the now-exes packed on the PDA while on vacation in Spain this past August.
