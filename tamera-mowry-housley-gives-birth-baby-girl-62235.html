Publish date: 2024-09-01

Tamera Mowry (Tamera Campbell)

In addition to Sister, Sister, the mom-of-two — she has a son Aden and a daughter Ariah with husband Adam Housley — has starred in Twitches, Twitches Too and Strong Medicine. Tamera, 38, is now a co-host on the daytime talk show The Real. She and her twin sister, Tia, also appeared on their own reality show, Tia & Tamera, from 2011 until 2013.
