Margot Robbie Reveals She Still Sleeps With Her Favorite Stuffed Toy Shes Had Since Birth!

Publish date: 2024-09-01

The actress wasn’t ashamed to admit that even now, she still sleeps with her childhood stuffed toy – a bunny! Margot Robbie was on the first episode of W magazine’s podcast Five Things with Lynn Hirschberg when she discussed some of her best childhood memories and more!

Of course, she also talked about working on Bombshell alongside Nicole Kidman and Charlize Theron!

The new podcast has a simple premise!

During every episode, one star details a person, place, thing, a positive event and a negative one that might have shaped them as people.

That being said, when it came time for Margot to mention an object, she talked about her stuffed animal.

According to the actress, the toy rabbit is appropriately named Bunny and she’s had it since birth!

But how does her husband Tom Ackerley feel about her still sleeping with Bunny at night?

Margot explained that he’s not the biggest fan, saying that he ‘always throws Bunny out of bed because he thinks it’s a bit ridiculous that I still sleep with Bunny. Maybe he is a little jealous Bunny always gets priority place in the bed. He thinks I am asleep and he’ll throw Bunny out of bed and I get very cross with him.’

The star went on to talk about getting starstruck by Nicole Kidman on the set of their new film.

‘I remember thinking, ‘Holy s**t, that’s why she is Nicole Kidman. The second they called action, she became this crazy presence. I could not take my eyes off her. Everything that she said, even if it was not a terribly profound line it just felt powerful. And I was like woah. It was very cool to witness first-hand. I remember just thinking like, ‘Okay, I get it. I get why she’s who she is because that’s magical.’’
