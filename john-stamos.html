Career

From 1982 until 1984, John Stamos appeared on the soap opera “General Hospital.” Soon after, he made an appearance in “Dreams.” He made a fleeting appearance in the TV movie “Alice in Wonderland” in 1985. Later, he had an appearance in “You Again?” as Matt Willows. He then took on the role of Jesse Katsopolis in “Full House.” He was a series regular from 1987 until 1995. Additionally, Stamos has been in the television movies “How to Marry a Billionaire: A Christmas Tale,” “The Disappearance of Christina,” “Daughter of the Streets,” “A Match Made in Heaven,” “The Marriage Fool,” “Sealed with a Kiss,” and “The Marriage Fool.”

The actor appeared in the television series “Thieves” in 2011. He has also had leading parts in the television shows “Jake in Progress” and “ER.” A Raisin in the Sun, The Two Mr. Kissels, Glee, Secrets of Eden, The New Normal, Necessary Roughness, Grandfathered, Scream Queens, Fuller House, and “You” are just a few of his other TV appearances.
