Everything to know about Cailin Russo, the girl from Justin Biebers All That Matters music video

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Cailin Russo is a social media star who gained fame after appearing in Justin Bieber’s music video for his hit single, All That Matters. She was only 19 when she made her debut. Know about her age and birthdate. Also, have a look at her wiki type biography.

Cailin Russo Wiki: Age, siblings and early life details

Russo was born on December 17, 1993, in San Diego, California. She was raised in her hometown with her two brothers.

Before she was cast in Bieber’s music video, she was associated with the Osbrink Agency, which helped her in getting the role. She had no idea that she had been selected to play opposite Justin until the night before.

Source: Huffington Post

“I got the call sheet the night before the shoot and my jaw dropped when I saw that it said my name and Justin Bieber’s only,”

Russo said.

Even after getting the call sheet, Russo was not sure that she would be sharing the set with Justin. She was surprised when she found out that their first take was them kissing.

Source: EOnline

The scene definitely broke a lot of hearts, especially Selena Gomez’s, who had recently broken up with the singing superstar. Gomez even took to Instagram to express her feelings. She posted a make-up free selfie with the caption, “I thought he only liked the Latina category,” supposedly referring to Justin.

Following her successful appearance in All That Matters, she was signed by new Mark Models and Wilhelmina Models. However, All That Matters wasn’t all about fame and success.

Upon the initial release of the music video in December 2013, the model also received death threats but she did not stop. She went on to appear in the singer’s another music video, Confident ft. Chance The Rapper, in January 2014.
