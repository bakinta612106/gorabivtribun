This How You Slay a Baby Shower: Zonnique Pullins Wows Fans with Her Entrance at Baby Shower

Publish date: 2024-09-06

“T.I. & Tiny: Friends & Family Hustle” reality star Zonnique Pullins is preparing to become a first-time mom. She was dressed to the nines with her child’s father, rapper Bandhunta Izzy, at their daughter’s baby shower over the weekend.

Pullins, 24, rocked a blonde up-down hairstyle and a peplum dress with heels. Izzy, 23, on the other hand, flossed in a red and white polo top, blue jeans, red kicks and an iced-out chain.

Fans swooned over Pullins’ diva-luscious look that left the soon-to-be mommy glowing.

“Yessss Zonnique 🙌 , this how you slay a baby shower 🤗.”

“😍😍 That dress is cute.”

“Now, THIS is a baby shower look😍😍.”

Fans also pointed out how Pullins and Izzy’s baby shower looked well put-together compared to the shower for Floyd Mayweather’s daughter, Yaya Mayweather, who is having her first child, a boy, with rapper NBA YoungBoy.

“Yaya, this how a baby shower is supposed to look.”

“Giving more millionaire vibes than Yaya ever could.”

“Dear NBA YoungBoy… It’s this simple.”

Social media users didn’t miss the point that Izzy, the father of two other children, was in attendance for his and Pullins’ baby shower.

NBA YoungBoy was allegedly missing from his son’s baby shower for some reason.

Over the summer, Zonnique, the daughter of Tameka “Tiny” Harris, member of the ’90s R&B group Xscape, and step-daughter to rapper and actor T.I., revealed news of her pregnancy to T.I. during the Aug. 4 premiere show episode of FOX Soul’s “The Mix,” presented by Harris.

“I need another drink and a joint,” T.I. responded to the announcement. Harris already knew that her daughter was expecting and took to Instagram soon after to make the official announcement.

On Tuesday, Aug.11, she spoke with Atlanta Black Star to share how she’s been coping with her pregnancy amid the pandemic.

“I’ve never been pregnant or anything, but it’s an experience for sure, you know,” Pullins said.

“I always felt, like, it would be like a time when everyone gets to be a part of it, you know going to my doctor appointments and stuff, but I have to go to my doctor alone. I can’t even take Izzy with me, and you know that’s definitely different for it being my first time,” she continued.

“I wish it could be different. But other than that, I’ve been feeling well. I’ve never got morning sickness and things like that, so really, that’s all I can pray for is a healthy pregnancy.”

The two have yet to announce their daughter’s name, but Pullins wants it to be something she likes seeing, as she wasn’t always a fan of her unique name growing up.
