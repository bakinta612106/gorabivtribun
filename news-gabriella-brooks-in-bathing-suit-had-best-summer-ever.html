Gabriella loves doing pilates on the reformer. "It's the best thing I've found for my body," she told Women's Health Australia. "I find that it's kind of meditative when I do it, it's not just, 'Oh I've got to go work out now,' it's something I actually really enjoy. Ideally, I like to do reformer two to three times a week. At one stage I was overdoing it and trying to go every day, just because I loved it so much, but I think my body actually resisted that and it just felt like a bit too much, so I pulled it back, which is better for my body."
