Sonya Eddy Movies and TV Shows; How Much Was Her Net Worth?

Sonya ringed her acting debut with the role in a play named Zora Is My Name in 1990. She made her television debut with the show, The Drew Carey Show in 1995.

A year later, she made her movie debut with the comedy film titled High School High. She was popular for her appearance in the TV shows like Married… with Children (1995), Seinfeld (1997-1998), Primetime Glick (2001-2002), and Those Who Can’t (2016-19).

However, Sonya earned recognition for her main role as Epiphany Johnson on the popular soap opera, General Hospital. She featured in a total of 543 episodes in the show from 2006 to 2022. She also featured in its spin-off titled General Hospital: Night Shift from 2016 to 2019.

Sonya also appeared in a handful of movies in her acting career. Some of her movies are Blast from the Past (1999), Buying the Cow (2002), Seven Pounds (2008), and Frank and Penelope (2022).

Thanks to her long-haul acting career, Sonya earned around $5 million net worth.
