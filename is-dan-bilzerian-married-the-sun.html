Is Dan Bilzerian married? The Sun

Publish date: 2024-09-06

NOTORIOUS playboy Dan Bilzerian sent social media into a frenzy after he posted a cryptic photo on Instagram.

The pic, which went viral, implied that the infamous poker player may have tied the knot – but it was not all as is seemed.

Is Dan Bilzerian married?

The image, posted in July 2022, showed Dan arm in arm with a woman at a wedding ceremony in the French Riviera.

Dan was dressed in a black tuxedo while his partner, seen clutching a bouquet of flowers, donned a sage green dress.

The poker star captioned the pic – “I finally did it”, as he teased his 32 million followers that his playboy days were behind him.

Some users and famous faces were quick to believe the post.

“It’s about dam time,” one wrote as another added: “You know nothing impossible when you see Dan Bilzerian getting married.”

UFC veteran Mike Swick commented: “Amazing! It seems like the 100ft below the surface underwater epic proposal was just yesterday! Nice Dapper Dan! I know where the Honeymoon will be…”

While a fourth quipped: “Finally my biggest competitor is locked down.”

However, rumours were quickly dispelled when it emerged that Dan had been attending the wedding of friend, Bill Perkins, in the South of France.

Hi partner, who was dressed in green as opposed to white, was thought to be one of the bridesmaids.

Perkins, who is a fellow high stakes poker player from Texas, uploaded pictures from his wedding to wife Lara over the weekend.

In one of the photos, Dan and his partner can be seen in the background – quashing the rumours that Bilzerian had tied the knot.

Who is Dan Bilzerian dating?

Despite being pictured with the glamorous brunette on his pal’s special day, Dan’s relationship status remains unknown.

According to celeb suburb, his last relationship was reportedly with an American model named Hailey Grace.

Rumours suggested the pair began dating in 2020, but this was never confirmed.

She featured heavily in photos on Dan’s Instagram account during the quarantine period imposed as part of coronavirus restrictions.

The poker star’s other most notorious relationship was with former Hooters bikini calendar model Sofia Bevary.

The Mail Online reported in 2017 that the pair had dated in what was dubbed his ‘first monogamous relationship.’

What has Dan Bilzerian said on marriage?

Dan’s life has been characterised by his playboy persona thanks to outrageous shots of hot women and wealth.

From images of yacht parties and expensive cars to guns and luxury items the Instagrammer has often been pictured surrounded by a posse of scantily clad women.

The saucy selection of snaps tally up his views on marriage, which first came to light in a 2014 post where he labelled commitment “not smart.”

He followed it up in 2015 with a post captioned: “Marriage; ruining lives since Adam and Eve.”
