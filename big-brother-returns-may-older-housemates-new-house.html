Big Brother returns May with a brand new house

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Big Brother will reportedly return in May.

Our friends at Big Blagger confirmed the news on Twitter yesterday, claiming the series will return for its sixteenth run in just over a month’s time.

It is believed that the series has been brought forward to create a gap between the regular and celebrity series; traditionally on Channel 5, the celebrity series runs just days after the civilian series crowns its winner.

Source close to the show reveals #BBUK brought forward to create bigger gap between civilian and celebrity series

— Big Blagger (@bigblaggeruk) March 15, 2015

The new series is also set to return for a brand-new house. Channel 5 often reuses house layouts and makes cosmetic changes, but this series its expected a new layout will come into play.
