Carrie Underwood's Baby Son Isaiah Plays the Piano in Sweet New Photo

Publish date: 2024-08-20

What a little peanut! Carrie Underwood’s little boy, Isaiah, seems to be musically gifted like his famous mom. The country superstar snapped a sweet Instagram photo of her son playing the piano on Wednesday, Aug. 26.

“Oh, just playing my piano in my blazer in the yard…” Underwood, 32, captioned the shot, which shows only Isaiah’s back for the pic. And yes, the 6-month-old did strike up some tunes while playing outside on the grass.

Underwood and her husband, NHL player Mike Fisher, welcomed their first child together back in February. In June, the American Idol alum opened up about motherhood, and how her two dogs reacted to the new addition!

“The puppy that I thought would be super excited about the baby has been a little sad, I think, because his world has been rocked,” she told Billboard magazine at the time. “I thought he’d be the playful one, but he’s kind of ignored Isaiah. Then, the one that I thought was going to be my problem dog has actually been the super-sweet and cuddly one. We’re still figuring everything out. I feel like they have adjusted alright.”

She joked: “I think it’s getting back to normal. They’ll love him when he starts dropping food. They’ll be best friends.”
