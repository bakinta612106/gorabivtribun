High School Musical 4 Auditions

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Have you ever imagined yourself in High School Musical? Well now you can be! Disney Channel announced they are holding open High School Musical 4 auditions.

To apply you must download the Disney Applause App. The app is available on both Android an iOS. Auditions will be accepted April 18th – May 2nd of 2016. You must be between the ages of 14 – 17 and have parent permission to audition. You can apply from anywhere in the U.S.

Keep an eye out on the Disney website for the script you will use to audition, and who knows, you could be the next Zac Efron or Vanessa Hudgens!
