Top Facts about Carole King

  • Carole King has sold over 75 million records worldwide.
  • She wrote or co-wrote 118 pop hits on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • Her album “Tapestry” spent 15 weeks at #1 in 1971.
  • King was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1990.
  • She won four Grammy Awards throughout her career.
  • King’s songwriting partnership with Gerry Goffin produced numerous hits.
  • She was the first woman to win the Gershwin Prize for Popular Songwriting (2013).
  • King’s music has been covered by countless artists, including Aretha Franklin and James Taylor.
  • She began her career as a songwriter in New York City’s Brill Building.
