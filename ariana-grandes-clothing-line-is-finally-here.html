Ariana Grande's Clothing Line is Finally Here

Publish date: 2024-09-09

It was revealed in February that Ariana Grande would be releasing her own clothing line in a collaboration with UK brand Lipsy London. Today, fans are finally able to purchase and own the products!

Prices start at £6 for the heart drop earrings and go up to £68 for the cutout body maxi dress. The collection includes jewelry, skirts, crop tops, dresses, and more. You can even buy some of her signature cat ears for £12!

Lipsy London also posted a behind the scenes video from the collections photo shoot, featuring one of Ariana’s many dogs, Strauss.
